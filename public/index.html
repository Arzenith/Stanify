How to Play

Press the 0 Key to begin playing. The timer will start and you can type your guess into the guess box.

To skip to the next song, press the right arrow key. To hear the previous song, press the left arrow key.

To add a new artist, type in their name into the top left input box and press "Add" or the enter key. To switch to your new artist, click the dropdown and click the newly added artist's name.
